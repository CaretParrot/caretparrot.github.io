Premiere Program

Performers that give a public performance of any of my unpremiered works may receive reimbursement for their music costs, and may be eligible for additional compensation depending on performance quality. Please check The Composers Foundry for any music jobs that I post.

Please keep in mind that many of my unpremiered works will need to be revised before they get sent. This means that a performer requesting to premiere a piece may have to wait several weeks or months before receiving their music, depending on my personal schedule and length of work. If you wish to premiere a work, do not wait to contact me.
