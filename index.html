Rishi Venkatesh

Rishi Venkatesh is a concert and electronic music composer that has gained prominence through the performances of Adagio For String Orchestra & Harp and Fantasia For Viola & String Orchestra with the Blue Valley Northwest Chamber Symphony. He is also a two-time winner of the KMEA Jeff Jordan Composition Competition.

Rishi Venkatesh is also the founder of The Composers Foundry, an online community dedicated to fostering the development of music composition and theory amongst young composers, theorists, and arrangers. More information can be found on their Discord server and Instagram page.
